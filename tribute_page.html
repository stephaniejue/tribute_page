Biography:

Hermione Jean Granger is a Muggle-born witch and the only daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Granger. She was born and raised in London and was accepted to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry on her 11th birthday. She was sorted into Gryffindor along side her best friend, Harry Potter, and spouse, Ron Wesley. Hermione is frequently described as the "brightest witch of her age," but has many admirable traits. She is loyal, sassy, quirky, and logical, to name a few.


Notable accomplishments:

  • Fixed Harry Potter's broken glasses on the Hogwarts Express (First Year)
  • Calmed Fluffy the three-headed dog to sleep (First Year)
  • Identified Basilisk as cause of petrifications (Second Year)
  • Saved Sirius Black and Buckbeak from capture and death, respectively (Third Year)
  • Danced with Viktor Krum and told Ron off (Fourth Year)
  • Protected Dumbledore's Army with spellwork (Fifth Year)
  • Achieved O.W.L.s in 6 subjects and summoned a book about horcuxes from Dumbledor's office (Sixth Year)
  • Helped defeat Lord Voldemort alongside Harry, Ron, and others (Seventh Year)
